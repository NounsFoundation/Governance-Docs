# Action by Written Consent of the Sole Incorporator

## Nouns Foundation, Inc.

A Delaware non-stock corporation

---

**Date of Consent:** [DATE TO BE INSERTED ON EXECUTION]

The undersigned, being the sole incorporator (the "**Incorporator**") of Nouns Foundation, Inc., a Delaware non-stock corporation (the "**Corporation**"), acting by written consent in lieu of an organizational meeting pursuant to Section 108 of the Delaware General Corporation Law, 8 Del. C. § 108 (the "**DGCL**"), hereby adopts the following resolutions:

---

## 1. Acknowledgment of Filing of Certificate of Incorporation

**WHEREAS**, the Incorporator caused the Certificate of Incorporation of the Corporation (the "**Certificate**") to be filed with the Office of the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware on [DATE OF FILING], evidenced by Delaware Secretary of State file number [FILE NUMBER]; and

**WHEREAS**, the Certificate is in effect as of the date and time of such filing and the Corporation came into existence on such date in accordance with DGCL § 106;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the filing of the Certificate is hereby acknowledged, confirmed, and ratified in all respects, and that a copy of the Certificate (as filed and time-stamped by the Office of the Secretary of State) shall be filed in the minute book of the Corporation as part of the corporate records.

## 2. Fixing the Number of Initial Directors

**RESOLVED**, that the number of directors constituting the initial Board of Directors of the Corporation (the "**Board**") is hereby fixed at five (5), consistent with the Bylaws of the Corporation to be adopted under §3 of this Consent and with the range (not fewer than three (3) nor more than nine (9)) authorized therein.

## 3. Adoption of Initial Bylaws

**RESOLVED**, that the Bylaws of the Corporation in the form attached to this Consent as **Exhibit A** (the "**Bylaws**") are hereby adopted as the initial Bylaws of the Corporation; and

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Bylaws shall remain in effect until amended or repealed in accordance with their terms and applicable law, and that the Secretary of the Corporation (when elected) is directed to maintain the Bylaws as part of the permanent corporate records of the Corporation.

## 6. Resignation of Incorporator

**RESOLVED**, that, upon (i) the filing of the Certificate, (ii) the adoption of the Bylaws, and (iii) the appointment of the initial Directors as set forth in this Consent, the duties of the Incorporator are fully discharged, and the Incorporator's role and authority with respect to the Corporation shall terminate in accordance with DGCL § 108(b); and

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Corporation, the initial Directors, and any officers of the Corporation (when elected) are hereby authorized and directed to file any further documentation with the Office of the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware, the Internal Revenue Service, or any other governmental authority as may be necessary or appropriate to complete the organization of the Corporation, without further action by the Incorporator.

## 7. Delivery and Effective Time

**RESOLVED**, that this Consent shall be effective as of the date set forth above upon execution by the Incorporator, and that an executed counterpart of this Consent (whether delivered as an original signature or by electronic signature, including by means of DocuSign, Adobe Sign, or equivalent electronic signature service) shall constitute an original of this Consent for all purposes; and

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that an executed copy of this Consent shall be delivered to the initial Directors and filed in the minute book of the Corporation.
